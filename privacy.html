StudyLapse Privacy Policy

Effective 5 July 2026

Right now, StudyLapse works entirely on your device: your data stays on your phone. There are currently no accounts, no servers, no analytics and no ads. If that ever changes, this policy will be updated first — see "The future" below.

What StudyLapse stores

Your study sessions, subjects, streaks, stats, settings and timelapse videos are stored on your device. Nothing is uploaded. Deleting the app deletes all of it.

Camera

The camera runs only while you are recording a study session, and only to create your timelapse video. Footage is saved to your device and nowhere else. Presence detection (a Pro feature that tracks time away from your desk) is computed entirely on your device and no images from it are stored or transmitted.

What StudyLapse does not do

  • No account or sign-up — the developer doesn't learn your name or email.
  • No analytics, tracking or advertising SDKs.
  • No data collection of any kind by the developer.

Purchases

Subscriptions are handled entirely by Apple through your App Store account. StudyLapse only receives an anonymous confirmation that a subscription is active — never your payment details or identity.

Notifications

Reminders (daily reminder, streak warnings, weekly recap) are scheduled locally on your device. Nothing is sent to or from a server.

Widgets

The home-screen widget reads a small summary (today's study time and streak) shared between the app and widget on your device only.

Children

Because StudyLapse currently collects no data at all, there is no personal information from anyone — including children — to collect, sell or share.

The future

StudyLapse may later add optional features that do involve servers — for example accounts, sharing timelapses with friends, study leaderboards, or cloud backup of your videos. If and when that happens:

  • Those features will be opt-in — the app will keep working fully on-device if you don't use them.
  • This policy will be updated at this address, with a new effective date, before any such feature launches.

Changes

Any change to this policy will be posted at this address with a new effective date.
